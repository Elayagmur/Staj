Healthy Food
A healthy diet is one that helps maintain and develop general health, and it means the diet that leads to the improvement of the health of the individual. "Micronutrients" that the body needs in small quantities to promote its growth, and improve it, in addition to sufficient calories for the body. There are many innovated diets, whose owners are prepared with varied results, but they are not based on a scientific basis. These regimes create confusion about what is healthy food, and give unnecessary false impressions about what could be unhealthy. These regimes are fiercely promoted. For people who adopt a healthy approach, a healthy diet is not as complicated as some think, as it contains mostly fruits and vegetables, with a complete reduction or prevention of processed foods and sweetened beverages; and the requirements of this system can be obtained from several animal sources, except those who are vegetarian or vegan. A vegan diet needs to get vitamin B12 from a non-animal source. There are many guidelines published by medical and food governmental organizations; To educate people about what they need to eat in order to be healthy. This is in addition to the fact that food information labels on products are mandatory in several countries, in order to give the consumer the opportunity to choose what suits him best. Based on which one contains the healthiest ingredients. A healthy diet, in addition to healthy nutrition, includes daily exercise, and this method can reduce the risk of diseases such as obesity, heart disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, and cancer.
